# Weekly IM Coordination Meeting Schedule

The Information Management Working Group meets every **Tuesday at 10:00 AM** to coordinate data collection, reporting, and analysis activities.

**Meeting details:**
- **When:** Every Tuesday, 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM
- **Where:** ODPEM Emergency Operations Center
- **Virtual:** Link available in the Calendar

All IM focal points and partners are encouraged to attend. Check the **Calendar** page for the most up-to-date meeting information.
