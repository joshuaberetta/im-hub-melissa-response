# 5W Data Collection Form Updated

The 5W (Who, What, Where, When, for Whom) data collection form has been updated with additional fields for better tracking of humanitarian activities.

**New features:**
- Enhanced location selection
- Beneficiary demographic breakdowns
- Distribution tracking
- Partnership information

Please ensure all activities are reported through the updated form accessible from the **Forms** menu.
